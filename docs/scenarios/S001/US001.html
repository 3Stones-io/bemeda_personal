US001

Organisation Receives Cold Call from Bemeda Sales

Scenario: S001 - Cold Call to Placement
Participant: Organisation
Status: Implemented
Priority: High

Healthcare organisation receives initial contact from Bemeda sales representative introducing the platform's staffing solutions and gauging interest for further discussion.

📝 User Story

As a Healthcare Organisation (HR Director), I want to receive and evaluate initial contact from Bemeda sales representatives so that I can assess how the platform might address our staffing challenges and determine if further discussion is worthwhile.
Who
Healthcare Organisation
What
Receive Sales Contact
Why
Evaluate Platform Value

✅ Acceptance Criteria

Specific conditions that must be met for this story to be considered complete:

  • ✓ Sales representative successfully connects with the HR Director or decision maker
  • ✓ Platform overview is presented clearly highlighting key benefits and value proposition
  • ✓ Organisation's current staffing challenges and pain points are identified and documented
  • ✓ Interest level is assessed and next steps are clearly defined (meeting scheduled or polite decline)
  • ✓ All call details, outcomes, and follow-up actions are logged in CRM system

👥 Responsibilities

📞
Maria Rodriguez (Sales Rep)
Make initial contact, introduce Bemeda platform, assess organisation's staffing challenges, and schedule follow-up meeting
🏥
Dr. Klaus Weber (HR Director)
Listen to presentation, evaluate potential value, share current staffing pain points, and decide on next steps
💻
CRM System
Log call details, track lead progression, schedule follow-up reminders, and update opportunity status

🔗 Dependencies

REQUIRED Lead generation system with hospital contact database
REQUIRED CRM system for call logging and follow-up tracking
REQUIRED Sales script and platform overview materials
AFTER US002: Schedule needs assessment meeting if interested
