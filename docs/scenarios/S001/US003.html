US003

JobSeeker Discovers Platform Opportunity

Scenario: S001 - Cold Call to Placement
Participant: JobSeeker
Status: Implemented
Priority: High

Healthcare professional discovers job opportunities through Bemeda platform and initiates engagement to explore potential positions that match their skills and career goals.

📝 User Story

As a Healthcare Professional (JobSeeker), I want to discover and explore relevant job opportunities through the Bemeda platform so that I can find positions that match my skills, experience, and career aspirations in healthcare organizations.
Who
Healthcare Professional
What
Discover Opportunities
Why
Find Matching Positions

✅ Acceptance Criteria

Specific conditions that must be met for this story to be considered complete:

  • ✓ JobSeeker successfully discovers the platform through search, referral, or direct outreach
  • ✓ Platform presents relevant job opportunities based on skills, location, and preferences
  • ✓ JobSeeker can browse, filter, and search opportunities easily
  • ✓ Clear job details, requirements, and organization information are provided
  • ✓ JobSeeker engagement and interest are tracked for follow-up

👥 Responsibilities

🔍
Platform System
Present relevant opportunities, provide search/filter capabilities, track user engagement, and enable smooth browsing experience
👩‍⚕️
Sarah Johnson (Nurse)
Explore available opportunities, evaluate job matches, review organization details, and express interest in suitable positions
📊
CRM System
Track JobSeeker activity, engagement patterns, preferred opportunities, and trigger appropriate follow-up workflows

🔗 Dependencies

REQUIRED Active job postings from healthcare organizations
REQUIRED Search and recommendation engine functionality
REQUIRED User tracking and analytics system
AFTER US004: JobSeeker creates profile and applies

⚙️ Technical Requirements

Search Engine
Elasticsearch, job matching algorithms, location-based search, skill-based filtering
User Interface
Responsive design, advanced filters, saved searches, bookmarking capabilities
Analytics
User behavior tracking, engagement metrics, conversion funnel analysis
Recommendation
ML-based job suggestions, preference learning, personalized content delivery
