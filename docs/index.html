Bemeda Healthcare Platform

Connecting Healthcare Talent with Opportunity

Vision

To become Switzerland's leading digital platform for medical personnel services, seamlessly connecting healthcare talent with opportunity while ensuring full AVG compliance and exceptional user experience.

Mission

We transform healthcare staffing through innovative technology, providing organizations with instant access to qualified medical professionals while empowering healthcare workers with flexible career opportunities.

Strategy

• Dual-model platform: permanent & temporary staffing
• Medical sector specialization with FMH verification
• AI-powered matching technology
• Phased market entry across Switzerland
• Success-based revenue model
