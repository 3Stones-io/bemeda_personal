Scenario 2

Direct Applicant to Placement

Status: Planning Phase
Priority: Medium
Progress: 45%
MVP: Vermittlung

Streamlined recruitment workflow where candidates directly apply through the platform, enabling organisations to review self-selected talent and efficiently manage the hiring process from application to placement.

🏥

1: Organisation

📋 Process Flow

1
Posts job opening on platform with specific requirements
2
Receives notifications of direct applications
3
Reviews candidate profiles and applications
4
Shortlists and schedules interviews
5
Conducts interviews and selects candidate
6
Completes hiring process and onboarding
