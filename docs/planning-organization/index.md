# Planning & Organization

## Overview

The Planning & Organization phase covers project management, timeline definition, resource allocation, quality assurance planning, and deployment strategy for BemedaPersonal. This ensures systematic project execution with clear milestones, risk mitigation, and quality delivery.

---

### [Project Plan](project-plan.md) 📝
**Timeline, phases, and deliverables**
- Project phases and milestone definitions
- Resource allocation and timeline planning
- Dependencies and critical path analysis
- Budget planning and cost estimation

### [Risk Assessment](risk-assessment.md) 📝
**Potential obstacles and mitigation strategies**
- Technical, business, and compliance risk identification
- Risk probability and impact assessment
- Mitigation strategies and contingency planning
- Risk monitoring and response procedures

### [Resource Planning](resource-planning.md) 📝
**Team allocation and skill requirements**
- Team structure and role definitions
- Skill gap analysis and hiring requirements
- Contractor and consultant resource planning

### [Quality Assurance Plan](quality-assurance.md) 📝
**Testing approach and quality standards**
- Testing strategy and methodology
- Quality gates and acceptance criteria
- Test automation and continuous integration
- Compliance validation and audit preparation

### [Deployment Strategy](deployment-strategy.md) 📝
**Release planning and rollout approach**
- Deployment pipeline and automation
- Environment strategy (dev, staging, production)
- Release management and rollback procedures
- Go-live planning and post-deployment support

## Project Management Framework

### Methodology
- **Agile Development**: 2-week sprints with continuous delivery
- **Risk-Driven Planning**: Regular risk assessment and mitigation
- **Stakeholder-Centric**: Regular communication and feedback integration
- **Compliance-First**: Regulatory requirements built into every phase

### Success Metrics
- **Timeline Adherence**: 95% milestone completion on schedule
- **Budget Control**: Within 10% of approved budget
- **Quality Standards**: Zero critical defects in production
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: 90%+ satisfaction in quarterly reviews
