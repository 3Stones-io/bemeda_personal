1
Initial Contact

2
Agreement & Job Posting

3
Candidate Notification

4
Candidate Review
Reviews candidate profiles, verifies credentials, and initiates contact through platform
5
Communication
Engages with candidates via integrated chat (with email notifications) or directly via phone/email
6
Follow-up & Closure
Receives follow-up email after one week asking "Did the deal happen?" with Yes/No buttons for confirmation
7
Billing
If successful, receives invoice with flat-rate amount. If unsuccessful, receives status update and hiring continuation query

Scenario 2: Urgent Temporary Staffing Need

1
Urgent Request
Hospital faces sudden staff shortage due to illness/vacation and needs immediate temporary coverage
2
Platform Access
Logs into Bemeda platform and posts urgent temporary position with specific shift requirements
3
Pool Worker Matching
System automatically matches request with available pool workers based on qualifications and availability
4
Quick Confirmation
Receives instant notifications of available workers, reviews profiles, and confirms assignment
5
Compliance Check
Platform automatically verifies all compliance requirements (GAV, certifications) are met
6
Assignment Completion
Worker completes shift, organisation confirms hours, and billing is processed automatically

Scenario 3: Try & Hire Program

1
Position Planning
Organisation wants to fill permanent position but prefers trial period to ensure fit
2
Try & Hire Posting
Creates job posting with "Try & Hire" option, specifying trial period duration and conditions
3
Candidate Selection
Reviews candidates open to trial arrangements, conducts interviews through platform
4
Trial Period
Candidate works for 3-month trial period as temporary employee through Bemeda
5
Performance Evaluation
Regular check-ins and performance tracking through platform during trial
6
Conversion Decision
At trial end, decides to hire permanently or end arrangement, with platform handling all paperwork
