⏰ Temporary Worker

Persona Profile

Role
Flexible Healthcare Professional
Background
Nurses, Medical Assistants, Specialists seeking flexibility
Primary Goals
Work-life balance, variety of experiences, supplemental income
Pain Points
Finding reliable assignments, administrative burden, payment delays

Scenario 1: Weekend Shift Scheduling

1
Profile Creation
Nurse creates profile specifying availability for weekend shifts only, uploads certifications
2
Shift Notifications
Receives push notifications for weekend shifts matching qualifications and location preferences
3
Quick Application
Reviews shift details (rate, location, department) and applies with one click
4
Confirmation
Receives confirmation within hours, with all details and contact information
5
Shift Completion
Completes shift, confirms hours in app, and tracks earnings in real-time
6
Payment
Receives payment within 5 business days, with detailed payslip and tax documentation
Key Benefits
Flexible scheduling around primary job
Transparent rates and quick payment
No administrative paperwork
Build reputation through ratings

Scenario 2: Vacation Coverage Specialist

1
Seasonal Availability
Medical specialist sets availability for summer months when regular staff take vacations
2
Advance Booking
Receives requests for 2-4 week coverage assignments months in advance
3
Assignment Selection
Reviews multiple offers, compares rates and locations, accepts preferred assignments
4
Preparation
Receives facility-specific information, protocols, and orientation materials through platform
5
Coverage Period
Works full-time for agreed period, with platform handling all compliance and insurance
6
Future Opportunities
Facility can request same worker for future coverage, building ongoing relationships

Scenario 3: Career Transition Support

1
Career Change
Healthcare worker returning after extended break or changing specialties seeks gradual re-entry
2
Skill Assessment
Completes platform skills assessment, identifies areas for refresh or development
3
Graduated Assignments
Platform suggests entry-level temporary assignments to rebuild confidence and skills
4
Experience Building
Takes various short-term assignments to explore different departments and facilities
5
Performance Tracking
Builds portfolio of positive ratings and references from multiple facilities
6
Permanent Opportunity
Leverages temporary experience and ratings to secure permanent position when ready
